# NovelRyt

## Tagline

Write Stories. Let AI Manage Everything Else.

---

# Executive Summary

NovelRyt is an AI-assisted novel writing platform designed to eliminate the administrative burden of storytelling.

Traditional writing applications require authors to manually maintain:

- Character databases
- Story bibles
- Relationship maps
- Timelines
- Worldbuilding records
- Lore documentation
- Plot trackers

NovelRyt automatically creates and maintains these systems by continuously analyzing the manuscript.

The author focuses on writing.

NovelRyt focuses on understanding, organizing, and assisting.

---

# Vision

Build the most intelligent story-development environment for fiction writers.

NovelRyt should function as:

- Writing Studio
- Story Planner
- Story Bible
- Character Manager
- Worldbuilding System
- Timeline Manager
- AI Writing Assistant

within a single integrated platform.

---

# Mission

Enable authors to write naturally while AI continuously maintains a living model of their story universe.

---

# Core Product Philosophy

## Writing First

Writing is the primary activity.

Everything else should support writing.

---

## AI As Assistant

AI assists.

AI never becomes the author.

The user always retains creative control.

---

## Automatic Organization

Users should not be forced to manually maintain planning artifacts.

NovelRyt should create and maintain them automatically.

---

## Transparency

AI-generated information must always be visible, reviewable, editable, and overridable.

---

## User Ownership

Users own:

- manuscripts
- notes
- metadata
- worldbuilding information

at all times.

# Success Criteria

1. Create a project.
2. Write chapters.
3. Receive AI assistance.
